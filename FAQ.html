FAQ

How do I mint Spooky Skeletoon?

You can get your Skeletoon by going to our mint page and minting it directly there.
You must have metamask installed and connect it to Fantom Opera network (how to: "https://docs.fantom.foundation/tutorials/set-up-metamask"). Afterwards go to mint page, connect your wallet and click mint. After transaction your skeletoon will be generated and you will be able to see it in the mint page shortly afterwards.

How can I trade my Spooky Skeletoon?

Lists of available NFT marketplaces will be posted on website and telegram as soon as we get approved.

Meanwhile you can transfer your skeletoons using transfer function in our mint website. Connect to your wallet, enter address that you want to send a skeletoon to, your skeletoon id and click transfer.
Alternatively you can also transfer skeletoons by going to token page on FTM scan, selecting Write Contract, going to safeTransferFrom, entering your address (from), recievers address (to) and tokenId.

How can I get Spooky Skeletoon with specific traits ?

Spooky Skeletoons are generated randomly. If you want a skeletoon with specific traits try to trade one on our telegram channel or supported marketplaces.

I just minted a Spooky Skeletoon and I can't view it in mint page.

Make sure you are connected to your metmask wallet and Fantom network.
After Skeletoon is minted it takes a short time to ganerate and upload an image. Give it a minute or two and click "My Skeletoons" again.
